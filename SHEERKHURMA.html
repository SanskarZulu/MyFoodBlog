SHEER KHURMA!✨

One of the most significant desserts of Eid ul-Fitr is sheer khurma. Sheer means milk in Persian and khurma means dates; it is a delectable and nutritious mix of sewai, dates and milk. This simple yet delicious dessert is versatile as it can be served hot or cold. (Read more)
